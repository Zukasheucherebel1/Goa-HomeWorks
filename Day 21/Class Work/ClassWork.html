BMW M5 F90

An exhilarating blend of power and luxury

Horsepower: 617 hp

Torque: 800 lb-ft

Mass: 1855 kg

Wheels: 20-inch forged alloy

About the Engine

The BMW M5 F90 boasts a twin-turbocharged 4.4-liter V8 engine, delivering 617 hp and accelerating from 0 to 60 mph in just 3.1 seconds. It perfectly combines raw power with refined control.

Chassis and Handling

The M5 F90 features an intelligent all-wheel-drive system with rear-biased handling, allowing drivers to experience a sporty feel even with high levels of stability and control on any surface.

Interior Luxury

The F90 M5 is crafted with premium materials, featuring Merino leather upholstery, high-resolution displays, and advanced technology, making it as luxurious as it is powerful.

Wheels and Design

With 20-inch forged alloy wheels, the M5 F90 sports a distinctive look, merging aggressive styling with aerodynamic efficiency, built to stand out while enhancing performance.
