Video and Audio Formats

Video Formats

  • MP4: Widely supported, high quality, and efficient compression.
  • WebM: Optimized for web use, open format.
  • Ogg: Open format with good compression and quality.

Audio Formats

  • MP3: Universal compatibility, small file sizes.
  • OGG: Open-source, good quality.
  • WAV: High-quality uncompressed audio, large file size.
