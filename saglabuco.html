BMW M5

Horsepower: 600 hp

Torque: 553 lb-ft

Mass: 1855 kg

Creator of the BMW M5

The BMW M5 was developed by BMW's M Division, a specialized team focused on performance vehicles. Originally led by Paul Rosche, an engineer known for his work on BMW's turbocharged engines, the M Division has continuously evolved the M5 to combine luxury with powerful performance since its first release in the 1980s.

Designer of the BMW M5

The BMW M5’s design reflects the work of legendary designer Chris Bangle and his team, who emphasized a bold and dynamic look. Known for pushing the boundaries of BMW’s traditional design language, Bangle’s influence led to an iconic, instantly recognizable aesthetic that combines sportiness with elegance.
